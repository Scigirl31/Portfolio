Bollywood Club at Midwood High School

Bollywood is the name of the Indian film industry.
Its similar to Hollywood. We plan to watch Bollywood movies,
listen to music, explore Indian culture, fashion, food
and celebrity gossip. We may also talk about other
countries like Bangladesh, Pakistan and so on. It the future
theres a possibly that we may even do a South Asian fashion
show! We hope to destroy stereotypes, and make a
community for those interested in South Asian culture.
